      Motherboard

      The motherboard is the main circuit board. It is mainly found in microcomputers and other expandable items.

      A cpu is the electronic circuitry that carries out the instructions of a computer program.

      A ram is a form of computer data storage. The two most used types of rams are the static ram and the dynamic ram.

      A hard drive is used for storing data on a computer. Hard drives were invented in 1956.

      The bus computer system connects all the internal components of a computer, such as CPU and memory to the motherboard.

      Keyboards are used for typing in words. Keyboards come with computer The first keyboard was invented in 1868 while the first typewriter was invented in 1714.

      A mouse is used for clicking on a computer. Mouses are very useful for clicking and more efficient.

      Monitors are used for observing, checking, or keeping a continuous record of a process or quantity.
